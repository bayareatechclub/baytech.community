Bay Area Tech Club is an Estonian non-profit association founded in early 2020 with the aim to help tech communities connect, educate and share their passions.

We offer software, infrastructure and resources, as well as guidance and best practices for self-organizing technical communities in an effort to help them operate successfully and sustainably.
